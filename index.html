CHOCOLATE CREAM AND COOKIE PARFAIT

Recipe By: Farheen Shadani

INGREDIENTS

  • Milk 1/2 kg
  • Cocoa Powder 4 tbsp
  • Sugar Powdered 12 tbsp
  • Cornflour/Custard Powder 1 & 1/2 tbsp
  • Butter 1 tsp
  • Cream 1 Packet
  • Oreo Biscuits For Decoration

PROCEDURE

  1. Step No.1

    2. Mix all dry ingredients together including Cocoa Powder 4 tbsp Sugar 6 tbsp and Cornflour/Custard Powder 1 & 1/2 tbsp

  2. Step No.2

    3. Boil milk, add dry ingredients and mix it well. Cook it for 2-3 minutes. Turn Off the flame and add Butter 1 tsp. Mix well and let it cool down

  3. Step No.3

    4. Whip the cream until it become hard. Add Sugar 6 tbsp and again Whip it until sugarr get melt and it become hard.

  4. Step No.4

    5. Remove Cream from the biscuits and crush them.

  5. Step No.5

    6. Assemble the ingredients according to your own wish. Firstly make a layer of Crushed biscuits, Secondly make a layer of chocolate and Lastly make a layer of cream. Repeat the process until the glass get full.


NOTE:

4 servings can b prepared with this quantity of ingredients.
